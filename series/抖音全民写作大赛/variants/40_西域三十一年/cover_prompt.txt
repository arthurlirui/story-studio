**Ban Chao (班超) - The Pacification of the Western Regions**
Masterwork ink wash and meticulous brush hybrid painting, dark atmospheric tones, epic cinematic lighting, dramatic chiaroscuro. A resolute middle-aged Han dynasty general in ornate Han-style armor and flowing silk robes, standing atop windswept desert dunes at dusk, one hand gripping a sword at his waist, the other holding an imperial edict scroll. Behind him, a small contingent of battle-worn Han cavalry with billowing banners and dust-covered horses. In the distance, silhouettes of the Pamir Mountains and a crimson sun sinking into the sands, a falcon circling overhead. Withered tumbleweed rolling across the foreground. Dynamic brush strokes blending wet ink washes with precise gongbi linework on weathered silk texture, calligraphic energy, golden age of the Silk Road, historical gravitas, 4K masterpiece.

**备用场景（不入虎穴，焉得虎子）**
Cinematic historical ink-wash painting, dark and foreboding atmosphere, epic scale. Ban Chao and thirty-six warriors standing at the edge of a barbarian camp under a moonless night sky, torchlight flickering on their determined faces. Black smoke rising from tents in the background. Bold splashed ink technique for the night sky, fine meticulous brushwork for the figures' expressions and armor details. Tense, heroic, a defining moment of courage. Ancient silk texture, film grain, low-key chiaroscuro, museum-quality historical epic.
